Block chain, promises and challenges

Blockchain is a distributed database that allows for secure, transparent and tamper-proof transactions. It is often described as a “digital ledger” and has been gaining popularity in a variety of industries for its potential to streamline processes and increase security. In the context of cybersecurity, blockchain has been touted as a solution for various issues, such as data breaches, identity theft and cyber attacks. While there are many potential uses for blockchain in cybersecurity, there are also some challenges that need to be addressed before it can be widely adopted. This paper will discuss the benefits and challenges of using blockchain in cybersecurity and explore some of the potential applications.
